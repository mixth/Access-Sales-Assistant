Objective: Determine why agency hasn't produced. Convince them to produce.

Overview: There are a number of reasons why an agency hasn't produced for us.

  1. They aren't comfortable with our program.
  2. They write little or no nonstandard auto.
  3. Our rates are not competitive.
  4. Our commission does not meet their requirements.

In your call, you will first want to understand why the agent hasn't produced. Depending on the Access TSM phase you are in, you will work your way toward gaining a production commitment from the agent. If a barrier exists that cannot be overcome, you should discuss next steps with your RD.
