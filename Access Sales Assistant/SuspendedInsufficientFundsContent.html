Objective: Collect funds due to Access.

Overview: Call agent to confirm that they have received notification from Access. Ask them if they are able to pay. If they agree, have them mail the certified funds to Access. If they havepreviously agreed to mail the funds, but haven't done so, make arrangements to visit them to pick up the money. Drop in as a last resort if necessary.

Questions to ask:

  • Did you receive notification that (# of payments) of your payments to Access was returned NSF and your appointment has been suspended?
  • What caused the NSF? What process do you have in place to prevent this from happening in the future?
  • Are you able to replace the funds? (Explain that we need a check or money order)
  • When can you mail the funds to Access? (Provide mailing address)
  • (If you need to pick up the funds in person) When can I stop by to pick up the payment?
