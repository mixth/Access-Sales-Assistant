Objective: Increase production or maintain the relationship.

Overview: There are three types of calls under this category:

  1. Training (Phase I, II, or III)
  2. Production (Phase I, II, or III)
  3. Service (Phase IV)

In a training call, your objective is to complete specific training that is designed to increase the agent's comfort level with Access and result in more production. A production call is designed to understand why the agent is not producing at an acceptable level and find ways to increase production. A service call is essentially a maintenance call. You are there to protect and enhance our relationship, ensure that the agent has the necessary tools to maximize production and gather market intelligence.
