Objective: Collect valid Errors & Omissions declaration page.

Overview: Call agent to confirm that they have received notification from Access. Ask them if they are able to provide a valid dec sheet. If they agree, have them fax the dec to Access. If they have previously agreed to fax the dec, but haven't done so, make arrangements to visit them to pick up the form. Drop in as a last resort if necessary.

Questions to ask:

  • Did you receive notification that you E&O policy information is expired and your appointment has been suspended?
  • Do you have a valid E&O policy?
    (If yes) Is it with the same carrier?
    (If no) When do you plan to secure coverage?
  • When can you fax the declaration page to Access? (Provide fax number)
  • (If you need to pick up the declaration page in person) When can I stop by to pick up the declaration page?
